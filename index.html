Yuliy Schwartzburg, Ph.D.

Hi! My name is Yuliy and I am currently a Co-Founder/CTO at Rayform SA based in Lausanne, Switzerland. I received my Ph.D. from the Laboratoire d'Informatique Graphique et Géométrique at EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland. I got my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from The Cooper Union. I've had the honor of being a Fulbright Scholar in Japan. I'm interested in how computer science can aid in art and architecture, and have been working in digital fabrication and caustic design.
