Hi! I am a post-doc in the [Laboratoire d'Informatique Graphique et Géométrique](http://lgg.epfl.ch) at [EPFL](http://www.epfl.ch/index.en.html) in Lausanne, Switzerland. I got my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from [The Cooper Union](http://cooper.edu) and my Ph.D. from [EPFL](http://www.epfl.ch/index.en.html). I've had the honor of being a [Fulbright Scholar](http://www.fulbright.jp/eng/index.html) in Japan. I'm interested in how computer science can aid in art and architecture, and have been working in digital fabrication and caustic design. Reach me by sending me an [email](mailto:yuliy.schwartzburg_AT_epfl_DOT_ch).
